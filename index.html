DPS Stats Guide

Enter your weapon's modded stats from the Arsenal screen in-game.

DMG Damage — total damage per projectile after mods

MS Multishot — how many projectiles per shot (1.0 = single)

FR Fire Rate — shots fired per second

CC Crit Chance — as decimal (0.75 = 75%). Values above 1.0 mean guaranteed crits + chance for orange/red crits

CD Crit Damage — damage multiplier on crit (2.5 = 2.5× damage)

SC Status Chance — chance to proc a status effect (0–1)

Note: Values over CC 1.0 use a weighted average formula for orange/red crit tiers.

Probability Guide

Use this to calculate drop farming odds. For example:

"What are my odds of getting a 2% drop in 100 runs?"
Set x = 2, y = 100, solve for z

"How many runs for 99% chance at a 5% drop?"
Set x = 5, z = 99, solve for y
